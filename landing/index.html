About Us

Aniska Eksteen opened her first aesthetic salon in 1998 in Germiston, Johannesburg. She established Aniska Skin & Art salon in Nelspruit in 1999. With a line of qualifications it was no surprise when her Aesthetic Beauty Salon made the headlines in 2000. Aniska is a great proponent of ongoing training. As a master in permanent make up she frequently travels across South Africa, training, educating and certifying beauty professionals in new techniques and latest trends in the industry. With more than 20 years experience, member of the society of permanent cosmetic professionals and one of the founder members of a regulating body established in 2011, the Permanent Cosmetics Association of South Africa (PCASA) she has established herself as one of the industry leaders in permanent makeup and beauty. As a trend setter and forerunner she maintains the highest level of quality when developing new beauty products for her salon. As a consummate professional, she believes there is no such thing as too much training, and that inexperienced and unqualified technicians ruin the image and reputation of the whole industry. Always make sure that your beauty salon is properly accredited before making use of its services – and remember: bargain hunting might cost you dearly!
